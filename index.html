About Us

We are a student-led initiative supported by the National Honor Society, dedicated to providing free tutoring for students in need. Our goal is to build confidence and improve academic success through peer-to-peer learning.

Subjects We Cover

  • Math (Algebra, Geometry, Calculus)
  • Science (Biology, Chemistry, Physics)
  • English and Writing
  • History and Social Studies
  • Test Prep (SAT/ACT)
